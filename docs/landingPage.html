White Matter Segmentation Education (WiMSE)¶

A practical, hands on guide to the logic and practice of digital white matter segmentation

What is this?¶

This resource is a collection of jupyer notebooks notebooks designed to build intuitions for and provide training in digital white matter segmentation. The modern-day practice of digital white matter segmentation is as much about programming as it is about neuroanatomy, which is why the medium of jupyer notebooks has been chosen. In this way, users are able to view commented code alongside text explaining the context, goals, and implications of what is being done. However, there’s a catch to this!. Because interactivity is key to many of these lessons, you will often need to click the rocket-ship symbol at the top of the page to open a binder. This will allow you to actually run and interact with code in your browser!, which, in turn, will give you a better understanding of how the code is operating.
